Donate to LeTS!

Thank you for donating to the LeTS (Learn Through Stories) Foundation.


Please feel free to give as much as you wish. As a rough guide, here's what some of your amounts can deliver:

  • Rs. 10000: a set of four LeTS books each to four village libraries
  • Rs. 25000: partial cost of a children's workshop in a rural school
